About us

Nature's Best

  Cold pressed oil is one of the essential commodities being used daily for cooking purposes. The oil is being extracted from oil seeds like groundnut, sesame, coconut, etc. in the traditional Wooden Vagai marachekku are run by power (electricity). We are procuring raw materials directly from farmers, Government recognized commodity regulated market of Tamil Nadu and nearby States like Andhra Pradesh, Karnataka etc. with the A Grade quality and process them in our manufacturing unit.
  We employ this age-old technique to naturally produce cold-pressed oils by crushing oil seeds at room temperature and obtaining oil products through precipitation and natural filtration. Since oil is extracted naturally, oil seeds are crushed gently. This is done to retain the nutrients and flavours. VSK Gramam Products are indeed healthier than any other oils as they retain their original nutrients. It also contains antioxidants which are usually absent in hot pressed oil because of their exposure to high heat.
